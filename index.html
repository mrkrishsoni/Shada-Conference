About Event

ICCIDS - 2024

The International Conference on Computational Intelligence and Data Science (ICCIDS -2024) is a premier forum for researchers and practitioners to share their latest research findings and ideas in the field of computational intelligence and advanced technologies. The conference will cover a wide range of topics, including machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, robotics, and artificial intelligence. The conference will feature keynote talks by leading experts in the field, as well as paper presentations.

All submitted papers will be under peer review and accepted papers will be published in the conference proceeding. Both academia and industries are invited to present their papers dealing with state-of-art research and future developments.
